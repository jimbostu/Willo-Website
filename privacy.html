Privacy Policy
for SnapSauce

Last updated: April 23, 2026

01Overview

SnapSauce ("the App") is developed by Willo Idea Lab LLC. This privacy policy explains how information is collected, used, and shared when you use the App.

02Information We Collect

SnapSauce does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All user data (such as favorites and statistics) is stored locally on your device and is never sent to any server.

03Third-Party Services

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location data (based on IP)
  • App usage data and interaction with ads
  • Device information (model, operating system)

This data is collected by Google in accordance with their privacy policy. For more information on how Google uses data, please visit policies.google.com/privacy.

04Data Storage

All app preferences, favorites, and statistics are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data is not accessible to us and is not transmitted anywhere.

05Children's Privacy

SnapSauce does not collect personal information from children under 13. The third-party advertising service (Google AdMob) may collect data as described above.

06Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

07Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact jim@willoidealab.com.
